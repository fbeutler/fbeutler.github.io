BOSS DR12 data

The power spectra, correlation functions, covariance matrices, figures etc. related to the Baryon Oscillation Spectroscopic Survey (BOSS) data release 12 (DR12) can be found here. The raw data is also available on SAS. Here are the BOSS catalogues:

  • galaxy_DR12v5_CMASSLOWZTOT_South.fits.gz
  • galaxy_DR12v5_CMASSLOWZTOT_North.fits.gz

and here are the corresponding BOSS random catalogues:

  • random0_DR12v5_CMASSLOWZTOT_South.fits.gz (750Mb)
  • random0_DR12v5_CMASSLOWZTOT_North.fits.gz (2Gb)

and the Multidark Patchy mock catalogues:

  • Patchy-Mocks-DR12SGC-COMPSAM_V6C.tar.gz (15.6Gb)
  • Patchy-Mocks-DR12NGC-COMPSAM_V6C.tar.gz (41.1Gb)
  • Patchy-Mocks-Randoms-DR12SGC-COMPSAM_V6C_x50.tar.gz (370Mb)
  • Patchy-Mocks-Randoms-DR12NGC-COMPSAM_V6C_x50.tar.gz (1Gb)

If you are interested to get the power spectra from the mock catalogs above:

  • pre-reconstruction 0.2 < z < 0.5 patchy power spectrum
  • pre-reconstruction 0.4 < z < 0.6 patchy power spectrum
  • pre-reconstruction 0.5 < z < 0.75 patchy power spectrum
  • post-reconstruction 0.2 < z < 0.5 patchy power spectrum
  • post-reconstruction 0.4 < z < 0.6 patchy power spectrum
  • post-reconstruction 0.5 < z < 0.75 patchy power spectrum

and the periodic boxes used to produce the Patchy mock catalogues (these are galaxy catalogs with a selection calibrated to BOSS CMASS, the columns are x, y, z, vx, vy, vz, Vmax):

  • BOX1.tar.gz (25Gb)
  • BOX2.tar.gz (27Gb)
  • BOX3.tar.gz (25Gb)
  • BOX4.tar.gz (21Gb)
  • BOX5.tar.gz (12Gb)
