BOSS DR12 data

The power spectra, correlation functions, covariance matrices, figures etc. related to the Baryon Oscillation Spectroscopic Survey (BOSS) data release 12 (DR12) can be found here. Here are the BOSS catalogues:

  • galaxy_DR12v5_CMASSLOWZTOT_South.fits.gz
  • galaxy_DR12v5_CMASSLOWZTOT_North.fits.gz

and here are the corresponding BOSS random catalogues:

  • random0_DR12v5_CMASSLOWZTOT_South.fits.gz(750Mb)
  • random0_DR12v5_CMASSLOWZTOT_North.fits.gz(2Gb)

and the Multidark Patchy mock catalogues:

  • Patchy-Mocks-DR12SGC-COMPSAM_V6C.tar.gz
  • Patchy-Mocks-DR12NGC-COMPSAM_V6C.tar.gz(41.1Gb)
  • Patchy-Mocks-Randoms-DR12SGC-COMPSAM_V6C_x50.tar.gz
  • Patchy-Mocks-Randoms-DR12NGC-COMPSAM_V6C_x50.tar.gz
